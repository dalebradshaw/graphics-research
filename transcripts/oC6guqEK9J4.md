# Particle Flow Logo Animation in Blender (Geometry Nodes Tutorial)

**Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oC6guqEK9J4

## Summary
In this Blender tutorial we will be taking any logo, converting it to a bunch of particles in geometry nodes. Then animating all the particles to come together to form the exact shape of your logo.
